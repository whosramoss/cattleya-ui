### variants

#### default

The `default` variant is used for medium emphasis actions. It should be used
in place of a call-to-action button when the action requires less prominence,
or when there are several primary actions of equal importance on the page.
`default` is the button's default variant.

<Canvas of={ButtonStories.Default} />

#### outline

The `outline` variant is for low emphasis. It should be placed alongside
other button variants to surface less prominent actions.

<Canvas of={ButtonStories.Outline} />

#### success

The `success` variant communicates a positive or confirming action, such as
approving or completing a step.

<Canvas of={ButtonStories.Success} />

#### destructive

The `destructive` variant communicates a dangerous or irreversible action,
such as deleting a resource.

<Canvas of={ButtonStories.Destructive} />

### magnetic

Buttons with the `isMagnetic` prop follow the cursor with a subtle spring
animation when hovered, adding emphasis to the action.

<Canvas of={ButtonStories.Magnetic} />

### disabled

Buttons with the native `disabled` attribute become inert and communicate
that the action is currently unavailable.

<Canvas of={ButtonStories.Disabled} />
