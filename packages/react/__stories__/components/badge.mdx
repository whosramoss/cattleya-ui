### variants

#### default

The `default` variant is used for the most prominent tag on a page. Use it
sparingly to keep its emphasis.

<Canvas of={BadgeStories.Default} />

#### outline

The `outline` variant is for low emphasis. It should be placed alongside
other badge variants to surface less prominent tags.

<Canvas of={BadgeStories.Outline} />

#### success

The `success` variant communicates a positive state, such as "active" or
"completed".

<Canvas of={BadgeStories.Success} />

#### destructive

The `destructive` variant communicates a negative or critical state, such as
"failed" or "expired".

<Canvas of={BadgeStories.Destructive} />
